What you get

Four things off your plate.
Four things you gain.

1

You always know where you stand.

Your books are closed and reconciled every month, not stitched together in spring. Open your file on any given Tuesday and the numbers are real: what came in, what went out, what you actually made.

What that looks likeEvery account reconciled monthly · every transaction coded to the right vendor · personal kept clean of business.
2

Someone reads the numbers for you.

Clean books are the floor, not the ceiling. Each month you get a plain read: what’s trending, where money leaks, how your margins compare, and a short conversation about what to do next.

What that looks likeMonthly P&L and balance sheet · a CFO dashboard of what matters · industry benchmarks · a standing review call.
3

No April surprises, and you keep more.

We plan your taxes across the whole year, on live numbers. Estimates are handled before they’re due, and your entity, compensation, and strategies like cost segregation are structured to keep money where it belongs, with you.

What that looks likeYear-round planning · quarterly estimates · S-corp and reasonable-comp strategy · business and personal returns filed.
4

The busywork runs without you.

Payroll, bill pay, and invoicing are the errands that quietly eat your week. We run them on schedule and sync everything back to your books, so you stop being the bottleneck for money moving in and out.

What that looks likePayroll processed and filed, W-2s and 1099s · bills paid on your approval · invoices sent and chased · all flowing into your books.
Prepared→ Independent Review→ Partner Sign-off→ Filed

It’s a simple standard. Nothing leaves our office until three sets of eyes agree.
